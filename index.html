The first railgun into space

About: Our Mission. Our Vission.

Ylla Space, founded in 2022, is building the first railgun for orbital space transport. Our mission is to make space more efficient, affordable, and electric. Our goal is to use the power of railguns to shoot small satellites or equipment into space.

In our dream world space exploration is powered by electromagnets.

At the moment we are working on a railgun simulator that will help us build the perfect and most efficient railgun, that matches our desired max payload requirements.
